Plm Bom Preserve

Odoo version covered : 8.0

This module alows you to upgrade your BOM following new revision process.

If you have a component and one Engineering BOM related to it you will have engineering bom lines come from client and other lines that you can add manually.

When you go to create a new revision of that component the new engineering BOM is created with new updates.

All BOM lines added by you in the previous revision are copied in the new BOM lines.

So in the revised product you will find your new updated BOM linked.
